Purpose

TOPIKS is a content aggregator designed to serve its clients with a vast collection of resources, from news and documents to music, videos and even photography.

Key features

One of the main features of our project is the large range of resources you can choose from. Whether you hate politics or not, this is by no means any problem. You can simply choose to exclude this category from entering your feed! The same principle applies websites as well.

Another key feature is the freshness of the things you get to see. Up-to-date news, trending songs and videos and stunning pictures. Are you the more of the nerdy type? We've got some research papers as well!

Finally, all of this is free. No cents coming out of your pocket!(unless your pocket is broken, in that case you should really fix it)

Starting things up

The beauty of using this app is the simplicty. Never ever has it been this easy to view resources of your favorite domains or to exclude websites you don't like.

All you need to do is create an account... or don't! You still get to enjoy access to some of our available categories. Either way, making a new account is really easy.

Click on the Account button located in the left menu. You'll get redirected to a page where you have to input your desired username and password.

Now that you've created an account you can access Preferences and customize your favorite domains or exclude some websites. Just choose your desired domains from the highlighted dropdown list to add or click on the remove button to undo. To exclude websites from your feed, type them into the textbox bellow.

To view your feed, simply go to Feed! You can even get your feed in RSS format by clicking the RSS icon located at the right-top side.

If you wish to delete your account, access Account, there you'll have to input your password and click Delete Account.

Administration module

The whole module is based on API calls. The whole documentation is available in the [References] section.

As an admin, you can manage or export users and resources and you can disable various user functionalities.
